Clinical Protocols

Hydration

Elderly patients often lose their thirst reflex. Dehydration is the #1 cause of acute confusion. Ensure Assisted Hydration (250ml) is performed during every morning window to maintain physiological baseline.

Neurological

Memory recall exercises should be performed between 02:00 PM and 04:00 PM. This is the typical window of peak secondary alertness before late-afternoon 'Sundowning' symptoms may begin to manifest.

Airway

Professional posture management is key. If breathing becomes shallow, verify that Resident A is seated at a minimum 45-degree angle. This protocol is critical for preventing secondary respiratory stress.

Critical Alert If a "Swallowing Difficulty" log is entered, all oral medication must be suspended until a physician review of dysphagia risks.

Ethics & Integrity

Data integrity is clinical integrity. CareNest operates on a Zero-Tamper Policy. Every handover note is digitally signed and timestamped. When logging, remember the clinical gold standard: "If it wasn't logged, it wasn't done."
